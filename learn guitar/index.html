Teach Yourself The Guitar

What Kind of Guitar Should You Get?

  • Electric

    Electric guitars have a lot of versatility, but require additional equipment to be played.

  • Acoustic

    Acoustic Guitars are cheaper, but they are limited to that sound

  • Does it Matter?

    Pick a guitar used in the genre you want to play, you can learn on both types the same. You will get more guitars as you play.

Learn Technique

Once you've gotten a guitar you might be tempted to look up a youtube video to play your favorite song, but while this isn't wrong you will find yourself frustrtated when you realise the song is probably harder than you thought. I recommend you first practice your technique and memorize the fretboard. Let me tell ya, it will seem smaller as you get better. I recommend looking up the Spider Exercises

Guitar Tab

Music Theory

If you want to get even better at guitar, learning some level of music theory can help, Look up some scales on guitar, they all have the same shape, and any note on those scales is fair game when making a riff, chord progression, or solo
